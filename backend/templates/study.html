Uber Usage Study

Welcome!

Thank you for your interest in our study! We are conducting a research study to evaluate the Uber pricing algorithm. To be eligible to participate in this study, you need to have a laptop, use Chrome browser, and be an active Uber user.

Note: You need to install a Chrome browser extension, login to your Uber account to provide data for this study. We DO NOT collect or store any of your login or personal information. The code for the study is open sourced here.

If you have any questions or concerns about the study, please contact Prof. Kiran Garimella (kiran.garimella@rutgers.edu).


How to participate

  1. Open the extension on the Chrome webstore.
  2. Click on Add to Chrome button.
  3. Click on Add extension.
  4. Open https://riders.uber.come/trips and log in to your Uber account.
  5. Enter your prolific study id and click Proceed.
  6. Click on Start data export to begin the process.
  7. The extension will begin to do its work.
  8. Click on the Complete Study button to complete your data submission.
